# Previous-year paper archive

The **Previous-year papers** screen contains a curated ten-year directory for
UGC-NET Computer Science & Applications (Code 087), covering 2015-2024.

The 17 paper PDFs are bundled with this PWA and pre-cached during installation,
so they can be opened without a network connection after the update completes.
The source for every paper remains visible in the app; the papers are not
presented as app-created questions.

## Format note

The UGC archive papers through 2018 use the former Paper I / Paper II / Paper
III structure. From the NTA CBT-era papers onwards, preparation should follow
the current combined Paper 1 (50 questions) and Paper 2 (100 questions) format.

## Source checking

- 2015-2018: UGC-NET archive pages
- 2021: National Testing Agency-hosted PDF
- 2019, 2020, 2022-2024: publisher-hosted question-paper copies, explicitly
  labelled in the app

Always consult an official final answer key when using a non-officially hosted
copy for scoring.

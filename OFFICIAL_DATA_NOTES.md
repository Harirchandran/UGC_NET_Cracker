# Data and provenance notes

The app embeds a structured snapshot of UGC-NET Paper 1 and Computer Science & Applications Code 87 syllabus data, examination configuration, historical cutoff references and a 2015–2024 question archive.

## Content labels

- **Original practice:** authored for this app; not represented as an official paper item.
- **Archived official-paper item:** a question record originating from an official paper/source archive.
- **Answer status:** states whether and how the official final-key option was mapped, including multiple accepted answers or dropped questions.
- **Content status:** independently states whether the display uses checked native text, structured transcription, manual semantic reconstruction or exact source-vector preservation.
- **Officially dropped:** retained for provenance and browsing, but excluded from scored tests.

A mapped answer index does not imply that every character of the transcription was manually proofread. Formula- or layout-sensitive content uses safe inline SVG when text conversion could change meaning.

Official source pages remain recorded for optional online verification, but the application does not depend on them during offline use.
